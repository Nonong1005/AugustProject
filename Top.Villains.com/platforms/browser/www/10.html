TOP 10

ACCELERATOR

(from Toaru Majutsu no Index)

Appearance

Accelerator appears as a skinny teen and has pale skin, moppy white hair and red eyes. This is caused by his ability, as it blocks ultraviolet rays, thereby preventing his body from producing melanin. His ability also reduces the outside influence so much tha it causes a loss of balance in his hormones, giving him what he calls an "androgynous appearance," although there is definite evidence stated throughout the series that he is a boy.

Background

Much of Accelerator's past is unknown, but he mentioned that he once had a "normal name" which has yet to be revealed, and most likely never will, as Accelerator himself forgot it. The novels state that his last name consists of two kanji and his first name three, yet his name had no real meaning in it.

Ever since taking the Power Curriculum Program, and realizing his powers, Accelerator was sent to a "special class", most likely, the Special Ability Institute, and spent much of his childhood there until nine years old, before being moved into another special class. Both of these had him as the only student. It can be presumed that this occurred before the incident involving the military.

Why he's on the list?

He has the ability to control vectors and influence objects that have vectors such as bullets, heat, and electricity. Also with his incredible mind, he was able to use his ability in different ways.
